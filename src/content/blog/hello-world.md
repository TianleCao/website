## Transitioning to Astro

After using WordPress for a while, I realized it was a bit too heavy for my needs. As someone who works with code daily, I wanted a more streamlined way to share my learning journey and research updates.

Astro allows me to:
- Write in **Markdown** and **MDX**.
- Render beautiful math like $L = \| \mathbf{A}x - y \|_2^2$.
- Keep my site **lightning fast**.

## Research & Code

For my technical posts, I'll typically provide a high-level explanation here and link to a **complete, runnable repository** on GitHub for the full implementation.

For example, you might see something like:
$$ \arg\min_x \frac{1}{2} \| \mathbf{A}x - y \|_2^2 + \lambda \mathcal{R}(x) $$
