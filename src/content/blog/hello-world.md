## Transitioning to Astro

After using WordPress for a while, I realized it was a bit too heavy for my needs. As someone who works with code daily, I wanted a more streamlined way to share my learning journey and research updates.

Astro allows me to:
- Write in **Markdown**.
- Keep my site **lightning fast**.
- Use **Git** for version control.

## What to expect

I plan to post about:
- Medical imaging and reconstruction.
- Computer vision and deep learning.
- Robotics and algorithm engineering.

Stay tuned for more!
