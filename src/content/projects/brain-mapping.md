![Brain Mapping](/images/brain_maps.png)

A novel technique for 3D multi-parametric mapping with whole-brain coverage based on MR Multitasking. It provides quantification results that agree with conventional references and has the potential for contrast-weighted image synthesis.

- **Paper:** [Three-dimensional simultaneous brain mapping of T1, T2, T2*, and magnetic susceptibility with MR Multitasking](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/mrm.29059) (Magnetic Resonance in Medicine)\
- **Presentation:** [ISMRM 2021 Oral Presentation](https://archive.ismrm.org/2021/1233.html)
