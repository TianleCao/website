A novel "joint update" approach for low-rank tensor image reconstruction in multi-dimensional MRI. Unlike the "fixed basis" strategy that uses subject-specific navigator data, this approach improves precision and repeatability for quantitative imaging while maintaining comparable quantitative measurement and septum sharpness.

- **Paper:** [Alternating low-rank tensor reconstruction for improved quantitative imaging](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/mrm.29878) (Magnetic Resonance in Medicine)
- **Presentation:** [ISMRM 2023 Oral Presentation](https://archive.ismrm.org/2023/0941.html)
- **Review:** [Highlights in Cardiovascular MRI 2024 Webinar](https://vimeo.com/905844343) (Review by Dr. Dan Ennis)
